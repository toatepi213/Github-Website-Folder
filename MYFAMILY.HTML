Family is Forever

ANA & SAMIU

Ana is remembered as a loving and kind soul with a strong faith in God, deeply cherished her children and held education in high regard. She was married to Samiu Moala of Vaini and blessed with eight childrenâ€”seven daughters and one son. In addition to her roles as a mother and grandmother, she found joy in weaving, gardening, and maintaining a clean home. Known for her unwavering belief in her grandchildren, even the mischievous ones, she often reassured others that every individual has their time to mature and become a pillar of responsibility for others. Her legacy continues to inspire, grounded in her love for family, faith, and nurturing spirit.

This website is to bring our family together

Click to expand this paragraph. Family is the foundation that shapes who we are, providing love, support, and a sense of belonging. Staying connected, even when miles apart, ensures that we continue to nurture our relationships, celebrate our heritage, and create new memories together. This website is a way to keep that connection alive, allowing us to stay updated on each other's lives and keep our family ties strong. As Proverbs 17:17 reminds us, "A friend loves at all times, and a brother is born for a time of adversity." Family is there to support us through every season of life, and staying connected is vital to maintaining that support.
